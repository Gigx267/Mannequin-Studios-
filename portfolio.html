American Express

We partnered with American Express to develop a dynamic social media strategy that resonated with their discerning clientele. Our approach emphasized authentic storytelling and user-generated content (UGC) to foster genuine engagement
This included visually compelling short-form videos, captivating imagery, and engaging micro-stories that showcased the unique travel experiences, exclusive rewards, and personalized service offerings that define the American Express lifestyle

We actively encouraged cardholders to share their own #AmexMoments, amplifying their voices and creating a sense of community around the brand. Our data-driven approach allowed us to track campaign performance, identify key influencers, and refine our content strategy for maximum impact
We conducted in-depth research to understand the evolving preferences and behaviors of American Express's target audience.
