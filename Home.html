Oye como va

Carlos Santana took rhythm and blues and gave it a Latin flavor. He also expertly complemented his extraordinary guitar licks with an array of percussion instruments to create a one-of-a-kind musical voice. Lots of influences are apparent in his music, especially jazz, blues and rock. In his early years, he grew out of the psychedelic '60s, but later he incorporated a spiritual element to his songs.
